Pricing

SUBMITTING THROUGH A CONNECTED PLATFORM

You pay a small AI drafting and admin fee — we generate your agreements, write your rights-holder outreach, and keep your document trail organized. The clearance itself is covered by the platform.

Not submitting through a platform? Use Cleared.live on your own. Submit your project, run the full clearance workflow with AI-assisted drafting, and manage every rights holder and document in one place. You're in control — we provide the infrastructure.
