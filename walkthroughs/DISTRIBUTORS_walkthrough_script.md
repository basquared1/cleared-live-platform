# Cleared.live — Walkthrough Script: **Distributors** (streamers / networks / indie distributors)

**Audience:** Distributor Business Affairs / acquisitions & clearance teams running full-service intake (`platform_mode = "clearance"`).
**What it teaches:** How a distributor invites submitters, sets its deal terms once, reviews and approves each clearance item, runs the publishing/sync negotiation loop, and green-lights a title straight into its CMS/rights system.
**Runtime target:** 5:30–6:30 (long-form credibility piece for BA / acquisitions decision-makers).
**Production method:** Narrated screen recording of the live app (capture at 1920×1080, 16:9).
**Voice direction:** Calm, senior, operator-to-operator. This audience runs volume — speak to throughput and control, not novelty. No hype.

---

## How this differs from the Labels script

Labels only **review and waive**. Distributors run the **whole** intake: they invite submitters, **set the deal terms** the submitter must hit, review every item, **negotiate** publishing/sync, and **deliver** the cleared title downstream. So this script leads on *control + throughput + deal terms*, where the Labels one led on *defensible sign-off*. Scenes 2, 5–7, and 9 mirror the Labels structure; Scenes 3–4 (invites + negotiation positions) and 8 (green-light/webhook) are distributor-specific.

---

## Pre-flight (do this before recording)

- Log in as a distributor BA on a `clearance`-mode platform.
- Stage the queue so it looks live: **one submission with all items uploaded** at *under_review* (your hero), **one mid-clearance** with an open negotiation, and **one already cleared** so the "Cleared" stat isn't zero.
- In **Settings → Clearance Requirements**, have your **negotiation positions** already filled (Primary + at least one Fallback) so Scene 4 has something real on screen.
- For Scene 7, have a music project with **publisher groups** generated and at least one sync thread mid-negotiation.
- Zoom browser to ~110%. Hide real PII; use demo titles.

## Exact on-screen reference (for accuracy while recording)

**Dashboard stats:** Submissions · Under Review · In Clearance · Cleared · Rejected (titled "Clearance Portal").
**Settings:** labeled **Clearance Requirements** — Preset selector (auto-fills form config + negotiation positions), Default Territory (lockable per field), **Section 2: Negotiation Positions** (Primary + Fallback 1/2/3: territory, uses, term, notes).
**Per-item actions:** Approve · Reject (with reason) · Log Response · Outreach · Draft · Download agreement (.docx).
**Music/publishing:** publisher groups (auto-partition by publisher) · per-group deal board (your positions vs. their offer) · AI negotiation loop (outreach → reply capture → counter draft) · MFN ledger across the group.
**Green-light:** flipping a title to **Cleared** fires `deliver_webhook` to the platform's configured `webhook_url` (enterprise: "Webhook delivery to your CMS or rights system" — dashboard.html).

**Routes touched (for whoever drives the capture):**
- `/platform/dashboard` → Invite Submitter → `/platform/settings` (presets + negotiation positions) → `/platform/project/<id>` → open item → **Approve** / Reject / Draft → music title publishing section (publisher groups → deal board → AI negotiation) → back to hero → flip to **Cleared** (webhook delivery) → **Agreements** tab → back to dashboard.
